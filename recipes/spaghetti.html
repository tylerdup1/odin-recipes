Tyler's Home-Made Spaghetti

This is my favorite spaghetti recipe that I have perfected over the years. I got this recipe
from my mother-in-law. It is simple, affordable, and very comforting. This recipe does not require
any fancy ingredients, just basic pantry staples and fresh vegetables.


Ingredients:

  • 1lb Ground beef
  • 1 Medium Onion
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 Bunch of Italian Parsely
  • 1 Small Can Tomato Paste
  • 1 Medium Can Tomato Sauce
  • 1 Medium Can Tomato Puree
  • A pinch of Salt
  • A couple spoons of sugar
  • A few leaves of basil
  • 1/2lb of Spaghetti Noodles

Directions:

  1. Chop the onion, garlic, and parsley finely.
  2. In a large pan, brown the ground beef over medium heat until fully cooked. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and garlic to the pan and saut√© until translucent.
  4. Stir in the tomato paste, tomato sauce, and tomato puree. Mix well.
  5. Add salt, sugar, and basil to taste. Let the sauce simmer on low heat for at least 30 minutes.
  6. Add fresh parsely and stir until well mixed.
  7. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Drain.
  8. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti.
